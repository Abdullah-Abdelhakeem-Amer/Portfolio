About aospcamp

aospcamp is a specialized training platform designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements in embedded Android and AOSP development. Whether you're an aspiring automotive software engineer or looking to transition into embedded Android development, aospcamp provides the structured learning path you need.
